How to Maximize Your Rebate

1. Provident Fund

Your 12% provident fund contribution is automatically counted as eligible investment for tax rebate.

2. Additional Investment

Invest in DPS, insurance, stocks, or government bonds to reach the maximum investment limit.

3. Timing is Important

Plan your investments throughout the year to maximize your tax benefits for the next financial year.
